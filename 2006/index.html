RED Formula

RED is a descriptive coaching metric. It rewards coaches who win more games than expected and do so efficiently.

RED = (0.70 × WinsAbove.500) + (0.30 × PythagoreanDelta)

WinsAbove.500 = actual wins − (games × 0.5)  ·  PythagoreanDelta = actual wins − expected wins (from point differential). Exponent: 13.91 (NBA standard).
