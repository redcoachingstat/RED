Why RED? RED answers one question: how dominant was this team relative to what their own stats predict? It combines winning production (wins above .500) with Pythagorean efficiency, giving a single era-neutral number that surfaces genuinely great teams — whether that's the 1975–76 Warriors or the 2015–16 Warriors. Historical seasons use: RED = 0.70 × WinsAbove.500 + 0.30 × PythagoreanDelta  |  2025–26 uses an enhanced formula with injury-adjusted roster expectations.
